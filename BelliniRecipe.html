After three drinks a person will start to lose their motor skills and balance starts to weaken
After four drinks a person will have slurred speech, blurry vision, and might have nausea
BELLINI RECIPE

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 oz Peach Shnapps
  • 3 oz dry Champagne
  • 2 oz peach nectar
  • 1/2 cup crushed ice

DIRECTIONS

Mix the peach nectar and schnapps in a chilled glass. Add half a cup of crushed ice, stir, and add the champagne. May be prepared in a blender to serve frozen drink. (Serves 2)
