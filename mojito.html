MOJITO RECIPE

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 oz white rum
  • 2 oz club soda
  • 6-7 mint leaves
  • 3-4 blackberries
  • 3/4 oz simple syrup
  • 1 cup of ice

DIRECTIONS

In a glass muddle limes, mint leaves, blackberries, and simple syrup. Add rum and ice, and two ounces of club soda. Stir and garnish with mint leaves and blackberry.
