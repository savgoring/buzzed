LONG ISLAND RECIPE

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 oz Vodka
  • 1/2 White Rum
  • 1/4 oz Triple Sec Liqueur
  • 1/4 oz Lemon Juice
  • 1 oz Pepsi or Cola

DIRECTIONS

Shake the vodka, rum, triple sec, and lemon juice with ice; then strain into a glass with ice. Add cola to fill, gently stir, and garnish with a lemon wedge.
